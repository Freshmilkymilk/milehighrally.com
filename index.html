Our Story

The idea behind Milehigh Rally was made in 2017. It’s creators, Michael Anderson and Bryce Salder, wanted a group of people that liked cars and could meet up to enjoy them. Milehigh Rally’s main focus is enjoying the cars it’s community owns. We do not care about how flashy or expensive a car is. What we care about is the driver experience. Local car communites did not focus on that so we created this community to serve those who want to enjoy their car with others that have similar interests. The owners of this rally drove their high school cars around Garden of the Gods and the mountain ranges of Colorado.

After growing up they move to better performance and more enjoyable cars. Now we want to bring this experience to others and create car meets, rally’s, and produce merchandise to bring the Colorado car community together. Milehigh Rally is a small group of friends but we hope to expand our reach in the future to have rallys all over Colorado and be sponsored by our favorite car manufacturers and companies.

Our Friends

Salt City Euros encompase our ideals and thoughts about design and how cars should be used. They are a YouTube channel with two friends just like us that want to enjoy their cars. The style of their videos is the same style we strive to have for our website and merchandise. If you want to know who the members of MileHigh Rally are like, watch a few of Salt City Euros videos!
